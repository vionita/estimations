Estimation And Scaling Frameworks

Context And Challenges

  • Multiple teams working on the same code base
  • Tens of developers, business and operations people
A single story point is unlikely to represent the same amount of work across teams and sprint

SAFe

  • Tries to *normalise* the value of a story point
  • Assign 8 points for every developer/tester in the team **BASELINE**
  • Find a small story that would take about 1 day (as team effort)
  • Call it a 1SP and estimate each story relative to that one

LeSS

  • Have a estimation workshop with all the team (or representatives) in order to have a **BASELINE**
  • Give it a few iterations to stabilize and then forecast
  • Rotate team members between teams to share knowledge and understanding
